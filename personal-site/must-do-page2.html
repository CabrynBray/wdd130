Check out the wild life at Pilaneberg national park. Pilanesberg is a pretty and picturesque park all year round. Virtually all of the animal species native to southern Africa can be found here, including the Big 5!

Enjoy the day at Sun City. Sun City has a unique heartbeat and an African rhythm of its own and is unlike any other. A family friendly place that has it all, The Bridge of Time, Valley of Waves, making it well worth a visit.

Have a thrilling adventure at Soweto towers. There is something for every adventure seeker, power and internal swings, abseiling, rap and base jumping, paintballing, and rock climbing.
